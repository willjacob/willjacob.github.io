About Me

Prior to Kellogg, Will was a Consultant with Deloitte Consulting’s Strategy & Operations practice. His engagements focused in Mergers & Acquisitions and Strategy, primarily for Health Care & Life Sciences and Consumer Products clients.

Will has worked in all phases of the M&A lifecycle, including pre-announcement due diligence, financial modeling and synergy analyses, Day 1 readiness, post-integration support, and divestiture planning. Will has experience leading senior-level executive interviews, conducting synergy analyses, modeling back-office stand-up and integration costs, developing business cases, facilitating workshops, completing functional integration planning, and writing corporate communications.

His transformational projects include an academic medical center acquisition that transformed it into one of the nation’s largest hospitals and Day 1 planning for a generics pharmaceutical merger that doubled the company’s size. Other highlights include developing a strategic business case for two large health plans considering a combination, conducting a strategic finance transformation assessment for an academic medical center, and supporting a revenue cycle transformation for a large community hospital network.

Beyond client service, Will is actively involved in the Boston community. Will was Co-President of the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation's Young Leadership Committee in Boston, working towards a cure for Type 1 Diabetes through targeted fundraising efforts and events in Boston.

He graduated Magna Cum Laude, Phi Beta Kappa from Bowdoin College with a BA in English and Economics, lending to his strong analytical and creative problem-solving skills.
